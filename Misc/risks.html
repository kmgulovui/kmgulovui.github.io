Technology Risks

First, in online education, it is difficult for students to be emotionally affected by computer screens, which means that studentsâ€™ emotions and thoughts are difficult to follow the teacher, and they are also easily affected by other things around them, such as mobile phone ringtones. The second point will also increase the time cost. For example, when there are any questions, the teacher will basically not answer the questions one by one in the online class. Students can only send emails to ask some questions, which invisibly increases some time costs. The third and most important point is that some teachers will only follow the teaching materials in the classroom, and will not change according to the characteristics of online teaching. Some education models may not be suitable for online, which leads to students The learning experience and learning effect are not as good as offline classrooms. I have surveyed some students, and about 70% of the students believe that online classrooms are not as effective as offline classrooms.
